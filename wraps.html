WRAPS & PPF

Unleash Personality. Protect What Matters.

At Micro Concepts, we turn vehicles into bold expressions of style—and shield them with precision protection. Using premium films and expert techniques, we deliver standout looks and long-term value in one seamless package.

Colour-Changing Wraps

Dynamic films that shift with light and angle - offering chameleon effects, pearlescent tones, and metallic finishes from top brands like 3M, Avery Dennison, and KPMF. Fully removable and perfect for personal style or brand flair. Specialty effects available on request.

Branding & Fleet Wraps

Built to boost visibility and recognition for single vehicles or full fleets. From reflective safety vans to campaign vehicles, we manage everything from design to installation—with eco-friendly film options available.

Paint Protection Film (PPF) & Protection Wrap Film (PWF)

Invisible, self-healing defense against chips, scratches, and UV damage—or enhanced protection with texture or colour. These options preserve your vehicle’s finish while elevating its appearance.

Whether it’s a daily driver or a masterpiece, we help your vehicle stand out—and stay protected.
