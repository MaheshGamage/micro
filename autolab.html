THE OTHER PERSPECTIVE OF AUTO CUSTOMIZATION

Welcome to MICRO Auto Lab

At Micro Concepts, we believe every vehicle tells a story—and we’re here to help you shape it. Our work goes far beyond simply applying films or graphics; it’s about transforming ordinary surfaces into powerful expressions of identity and purpose. Whether you want to turn heads on the street, protect your investment, or create a consistent and professional brand presence, we offer a comprehensive approach that merges creativity, precision, and lasting performance.

We take the time to understand not just the look you’re after, but also how your vehicle fits into your lifestyle or business goals. We consider everything—from the way natural light interacts with certain finishes to how your brand message will resonate on a moving canvas. This attention to detail allows us to recommend solutions that go beyond aesthetics, delivering practical benefits like durability, ease of maintenance, and long-term value.

COLOUR-CHANGING WRAPS

Express yourself with dynamic films that shift with light and angle—from chameleon effects and pearlescent tones to sleek metallic finishes. Using trusted brands like 3M, Avery Dennison, and KPMF, our wraps are fully removable and ideal for personal flair or business branding. Specialty finishes are available upon request.

PAINT PROTECTION (PPF/PWF)

Shield your vehicle with invisible, self-healing defense against chips, scratches, and UV damage—or elevate it further with textured or colored protection wraps.

From daily drivers to collector’s pieces, our films preserve your finish, enhance your look, and keep your vehicle turning heads for years to come.

BRANDING & FLEET WRAPS

Make your vehicles work for your brand. From single cars to full fleets, we design and install wraps that boost visibility, recognition, and impact. Options include reflective safety graphics, campaign wraps, and eco-friendly film choices.

DETAILING

For us, detailing isn’t just cleaning—it’s a complete restoration of your vehicle’s comfort, shine, and value.

Whether you’re preparing for a show, protecting resale value, or simply treating your car, our detailing makes it look—and feel—like new again.

At Micro Auto Lab;

We see customization as a collaboration. We don’t just provide a service—we partner with you to bring your vision to life. From the initial concept and design phase to the final reveal, we guide you through each step of the process, offering professional insight and creative ideas tailored to your needs. The result is not just a transformed vehicle, but an experience that leaves you confident, inspired, and ready to make an impact wherever the road takes you.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to craftsmanship. Every project we take on is handled with care, using advanced tools and installation techniques to ensure a flawless, seamless finish. By working with some of the industry’s most trusted and innovative materials, we ensure that every wrap or protection film we install performs beautifully under real-world conditions, whether you’re navigating the busy streets of the city or embarking on rugged adventures.
